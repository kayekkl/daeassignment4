什麼是開源硬體？ What is OpenSource Hardwarez?

開源硬體，即OpenSource Hardware ，是一個指有形的造物：機器、設備或其它物理東西的術語——其設計向公眾發布，任何人可以製造、修改、分發並使用那些造物。 」 隨著物聯網和電子技術的發展，現在越來越多的硬體開始開源了，也許在你身邊用到的某一塊主機板就是開源硬體。

Open-source hardware, also known as Open Source Hardware, refers to tangible creations such as machines, devices, or other physical objects whose designs are made publicly available. Anyone can manufacture, modify, distribute, and use these creations. With the development of the Internet of Things (IoT) and electronic technology, more and more hardware is becoming open-source. Perhaps a motherboard you are using right now is open-source hardware.
